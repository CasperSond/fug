User ID help

You can use the following as your user ID:

  • CPR number (only for citizens).
  • NemID number.
  • Your self-chosen user ID.

If you are logging on for the first time, use your NemID number found in the letter you got from your bank, Nets DanID or Borgerservice (citizen service centre).

"Remember me" saves the user ID that you have entered. This means you will no longer need to enter your user ID for the current service in future. You will always have to enter your password. If you want to use a different user ID, or if you do not want your user ID to be remembered, uncheck the box.

Password help

Enter your NemID password, which should be 4 digits or at least 6 characters (letters and numbers).

First time using NemID? Or has NemID been revoked? Enter the activation password you received:

  • at your bank, at Borgerservice (citizen service centre).
  • in a letter from Nets DanID.
  • via text message.

Did you forget your password?

If you have forgotten your password, you must request a new activation password.

Please note that NemID will be revoked when you order a new password. This means you will have to wait for a new activation password even if you later remember your old password.

Do you use personal NemID?

  • Request one at nemid.nu/password (opens in a new window).
  • Contact your bank.
  • Contact NemID Support.

Do you use NemID business for banking?

  • Contact your bank.

Do you use NemID employee certificate?

  • Request one at medarbejdersignatur.dk/adgangskode (in Danish) (opens in a new window).
